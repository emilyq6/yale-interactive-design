My Life in 24 Hours


Have you ever thought of your life as a measure of time and space,

Your relationships, your memories, all things that cannot be replaced.

When have you ever questioned the reason for 24 hours clocks

Or why the cycle stays continuous and never stops?

I asked these questions to myself as I reflected on my 16-year life,

My relationships, my memories, events that caused me strife.

I asked “what if our whole life was represented by just one single day,”

Facing each memory, each photograph and never pushing away.



My creation is a representative piece,

One that carries my life in with little words, to say the least.

This clock measures my day in photographed memories,

From birth to now, experiences that will never leave.

Photos appear within this new 16 interval day,

Chronologically accurate as each day portrays,

The cycle of my life in the context of today.
