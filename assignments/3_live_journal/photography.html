Emily's Photography

I've been passionate about photography ever since I was around 13 years old when my dad bought me my first Nikon as a birthday present. I became addicted to taking photos of people, plants, inanimate objects, just about anything I could find.
That amazing feeling of getting the perfect shot is something that's incomparable to anything else and throughout the years I've taken pictures for hundreds of athletes at my school, gone on dozens of shoots with friends, along with spending hours in my backyard searching for my next model whether that be a small little bee or a stray cat that finds its way into our gate.
I've chosen some of my favorite photos and I hope you enjoy.
